# CreditBuddy UK – Product Roadmap

## Phase 1 – Foundation (Current Stage)

Goal: Build the core platform structure and educational content.

Features:
- Landing page
- Credit score education hub
- Basic financial guidance pages
- Clean user interface
- Project architecture setup

---

## Phase 2 – Core Credit Tools

Goal: Introduce practical tools for users.

Features:
- Credit score explanation tool
- Credit improvement checklist
- Debt management guides
- Financial health assessment

---

## Phase 3 – Smart Financial Guidance

Goal: Help users make better financial decisions.

Features:
- Personalized credit improvement plans
- Credit eligibility checker
- Credit card and loan recommendations
- Risk indicators for credit applications

---

## Phase 4 – AI Financial Assistant

Goal: Add AI-powered insights.

Features:
- AI credit improvement assistant
- Financial question answering
- Personalized credit strategy

---

## Phase 5 – Platform Expansion

Goal: Become a full financial guidance platform.

Future ideas:
- Credit monitoring integrations
- Financial product marketplace
- Partner integrations with lenders
- Mobile-friendly platform
